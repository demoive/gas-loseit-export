The Lose It! `fn_auth` session cookie expires on . Update the `LOSEIT_SESSION_COOKIE` script property before it expires, otherwise the sync will fail. HOW TO CYCLE THE COOKIE ──────────────────────── 1. Log in to https://www.loseit.com in a browser. 2. Open your browser's DevTools (usually: Cmd+Option+I). 3. In [Application] → Storage → Cookies → www.loseit.com 3. Copy the Value of the `fn_auth` cookie. 4. Open your Apps Script's settings: 5. Under "Script Properties" paste the value for the `LOSEIT_SESSION_COOKIE` property. 6. Save.
